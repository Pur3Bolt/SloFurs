SloFurs Privacy Policy

This privacy policy will explain how our organization uses the personal data we collect from you when you use our website.

Information about us

The party responsible for this website for purposes of data protection is SloFurs.

Website address: slofurs.org

Email: slofurs@gmail.com

What data do we collect?

SloFurs collects the following data:

  • Personal identification information (name, email address, address, phone number, etc.).
  • Your current and past event registrations.
  • Your preferences regarding certain topics on some events (food and drink preferences, game preferences, etc.).

How do we collect your data?

You directly provide SloFurs with the data we collect. We collect data and process data when you:

  • Register online.
  • Fill out your profile with personal information.
  • Register for an event.
  • Voluntarily complete a post-event survey or provide feedback on any of our message boards (eg. Discord) or via email.
  • Use or view our website via your browser's cookies.

How will we use your data?

SloFurs collects your data so that we can:

  • Process and manage your account.
  • Generate release forms which you sign at the event, if required for the event.
  • Process payments online.
  • Email you with upcoming events held by us.
  • Email you with important updates for events you registered for.

A small portion of your data may be shared with SendGrid to have the ability to send you email messages.

No data is shared with or used by any other third-party websites or persons. The data you provide is available to the event organisers.

When SloFurs processes your event registration and collects payments, it may also receive and use the resulting information from the payment processor (eg. PayPal), such as your name, email, address, etc. stored on the payment processor. In no case does SloFurs receive or process credit card information.

How do we store your data?

SloFurs securely stores your data at our server provider.

SloFurs will keep your Personal identification information (name, address, phone number, date of birth, gender) from the time you provide it to us until you decide to delete it.

You can remove your personal information from the user account pages, however you will not be able to do so if you have registered for events that are upcoming or, if the last attended event is less than 5 days ago.


If you must sign a release form at an event, these are stored with the event organiser for a period of up to 10 days after the event ended.

Once this time period has expired, the organiser will destroy the release forms.

What are your data protection rights?

SloFurs would like to make sure you are fully aware of all of your data protection rights. Every user is entitled to the following:

The right to access – You have the right to request SloFurs to give you copies of your personal data.

The right to rectification – You have the right to request that SloFurs correct any information you believe is inaccurate. You also have the right to request SloFurs to complete the information you believe is incomplete.

The right to erasure – You have the right to request that SloFurs erase your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to restrict processing – You have the right to request that SloFurs restrict the processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to object to processing – You have the right to object to SloFurs' processing of your personal data, under certain conditions.

The right to data portability – You have the right to request that SloFurs transfer the data that we have collected to another organization, or directly to you, under certain conditions.

If you make a request, we have one month to respond to you. If you would like to exercise any of these rights, please contact us at our email: slofurs@gmail.com.

Cookies

Cookies are text files placed on your computer to collect standard Internet log information and visitor behavior information. When you visit our websites, we may collect information from you automatically through cookies or similar technology.

For further information, visit allaboutcookies.org.

How do we use cookies?

SloFurs uses cookies in a range of ways to improve your experience on our website, including:

  • Keeping you signed in.
  • Understanding how you use our website.

What types of cookies do we use?

There are a number of different types of cookies, however, our website uses:

  • Session cookies – SloFurs uses these cookies so that we recognize you on our website and remember who you are for the active user session. When you close your browser, these session cookies are deleted.
  • Third-party cookies – SloFurs uses these cookies to collect information about your visit to our website, the content you viewed, the links you followed and information about your browser, device, and your IP address. SloFurs uses these cookies for the purpose of analyzing or improving features of our website. SloFurs does not share this data with third parties for advertising purposes.

How to manage cookies

You can set your browser not to accept cookies, and the above website tells you how to remove cookies from your browser. However, some of our website features may not function as a result.

Privacy policies of other websites

The SloFurs website may contain links to other websites. Our privacy policy applies only to our website, so if you click on a link to another website, you should read their privacy policy.

Changes to our privacy policy

SloFurs keeps its privacy policy under regular review and places any updates on this web page. This privacy policy was last updated on 9 July 2019.

How to contact us
